Turkish Ice Cream form is really creamy, chewy, and intense. With these features, it is separate from the other ice creams.
If you like you can eat it with a knife and fork. Traditional Ice Cream vendors are also different than everywhere else. They are mostly male and wear traditional fez and waistcoats like they are from Ottoman Empire. They serve the Ice Cream in a very tricky way. Actually, we cannot call it to serve.
It is almost a show that seems like magic tricks. In Kahramanmaraş, Turkish Ice Cream has become well-known. And the greatest are always there, which is why there are so many Kahramanmaraş Ice Cream Shops in other places. However, they are not the same as the traditional ones.
Osman Aga from Maras discovered Kahramanmaraş ice cream by accident while making the orchid while on salep. After three generations, the dessert was renamed "Maras ice cream" from "karsambaç with salep."
The fact that Kahramanmaraş ice cream is made with goat milk is one of its most distinguishing features.

You Need to Know About Turkish Ice Cream

Turkish ice cream is rich, creamy, chewy, and flavorful. It stands out among the other ice creams because of these characteristics.
You may eat it with a knife and fork if you choose. Traditional ice cream sellers are also distinct from those seen elsewhere. They are usually men who dress in traditional fez and waistcoats as if they were from the Ottoman Empire.
They serve the ice cream in an unusual manner. We can't really call it serving. It's almost as if it's a magic trick show.

Turkish Ice Cream Vendors Tricks

When you visit an ice cream shop you will find a cart in front of it and a vendor knead and pound away at the dessert all day with long metal rods, working it like a bread dough ice cream in the cart,
The salesmen wear regional dress costumes from the Kahramanmaraş area which is the origin of Turkish traditional ice cream. And when you come to buy a scoop, the vendor break out their showoff and put on an act, utilizing the peculiar qualities of dondurma to perform a seamless series of magic tricks.
Now, you have to work for your cone while laughing and having fun. The vendor will start a competition between you and him, you can actually touch the cone or even hold it but never take it, every single time the vendor will trick you with a move of him.
He will let you think that it’s about to fall down, sticking it to your nose, and confuse you with his tricks.Whenever you think you are closer to his ice cream cone, the vendor would definitely fake you out with another trick. It is a lesson in patience.
You can also trick him if you want! Vendors are expert at tricking but if your less, you can flip the competition over and win the game.
